Manual Lymphatic Therapy

MLD is a gentle and specific technique designed to increase lymphatic flow. With an increase of lymph flow immune function is increased. The fluid in the lymphatic system helps remove waste, bacteria, excess fluid (swelling) and metabolic waste. An increase in lymph flow stimulates an increased production of lymphocytes, enhancing immune function against infections.

MLD is not a muscular massage as it works above the muscle fascia due to 70% of the lymphatic fluid being found there.

This therapy can be beneficial to those who suffer from lymphedema, fibromyalgia, swelling or edema, skin disorders, insomnia, stress, digestive problems, arthritis and other conditions.

Manual Lymphatic Drainage may be beneficial to:

  • MLD post surgery can assist in greatly preventing scarring of the area and enable the body to remove unwanted fluid at the site, thus preventing infection.
  • Pre-surgery MLD is an invaluable tool for softening and relaxing tissues that need to be operated on.
  • Edema (edema) in the sprained ankle and other post-traumatic conditions can be treated effectively with the immediate application of RICE therapy in conjunctions with MLD.
  • Lymphedema
  • Sinusitis/Hay fever
  • Breast surgery (segmental, simple or total)
  • Dentistry
